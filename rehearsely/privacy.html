Rehearsely Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Rehearsely does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All scripts, categories, tags, notes, and settings you create are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

Rehearsely stores app data on your device using on-device storage. The app does not upload your content to a server.

Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Rehearsely does not use analytics, advertising, third-party tracking, or cross-app tracking.

Internet Access

No internet connection is required to use Rehearsely.

Contact

For support, visit Rehearsely Support.
