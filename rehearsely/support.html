Rehearsely Support

Rehearsely is an offline conversation rehearsal app for iPhone.

Help

Rehearsely stores scripts, categories, tags, notes, and settings on your device. If you delete the app, your local app data may be deleted.

Refunds

Purchases and refund requests are handled by Apple through the App Store. Visit Apple Billing Support for refund help.

Contact

For support questions, open an issue at github.com/beoum-dev/legal/issues.
