Scriptly Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Scriptly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All scripts, categories, tags, notes, and settings you create are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

Scriptly stores app data on your device using on-device storage. The app does not upload your content to a server.

Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Scriptly does not use analytics, advertising, third-party tracking, or cross-app tracking.

Internet Access

No internet connection is required to use Scriptly.

Contact

For questions, open an issue at github.com/beoum-dev/scriptly.
